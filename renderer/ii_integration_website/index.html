Welcome!

Securely log in to DFX Dashboard using Internet Identity. Internet Identity is a free, open-source identity provider built on the Internet Computer.

Login Process

  1. Click the "Login with Internet Identity" button above and complete the authentication.
  2. When prompted "Are you sure to open with dfx://", click "Yes".
  3. The DFX Dashboard app will open automatically. Once you see your Internet Identity in the app, you can close this page.
